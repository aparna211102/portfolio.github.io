What is a portfolio?


The portfolio can have the same purpose as a CV. Most CVs are written with text, while the portfolio is for showcasing, so it is visual with images and often more detailed than the CV.

It is a place where you can show your work experience and showcase the projects that you are most proud of.

Your online portfolio can be shared with its link to companies, hiring managers, and recruiters, so that they can notice you.

It is about showing and giving others an understanding of who you are as a professional

Why you should have a Portfolio Website?

  • A portfolio website is your online résumé.

  • It is a great way of growing your online presence and get noticed.

  • Portfolios are a step toward a more public, professional view.

  • Give detailed description where one can present their personal, social, academic as well as extra activities without any fail.

  • To document student learning on specific curriculum outcomes.

What do I need to know to create my own portfolio??

    You can make a cool portfolio website for yourself with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


  1. Create the structure with HTML. The first thing you have to learn, is HTML, which is the standard markup language for creating web pages.

  2. Style with CSS. The next step is to learn CSS, to set the layout of your web page with beautiful colors, fonts, and much more.

  3. Make it interactive with JavaScript. After studying HTML and CSS, you should learn JavaScript to create dynamic and interactive web pages for your users.
